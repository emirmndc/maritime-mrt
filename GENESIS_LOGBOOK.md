## Purpose

The MRT Genesis Logbook documents the early formation of the MRT open market.

This archive records independent, on-chain buy transactions as liquidity forms organically.

---

## Rules

To maintain integrity and transparency, entries must meet the following criteria:

- Only independent wallets (no owner, treasury, or liquidity wallets)
- Only DEX BUY transactions
- Minimum transaction size: $1 USDC
- No manual allocations
- No internal transfers
- No wash trading

All qualifying transactions will be reviewed and archived once per day.
